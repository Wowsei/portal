Xmove - Train & Bus Booking

The ultimate Europe and Asia railway train booking app. Xmove offers a one-stop solution for international train ticket search and booking, covering over 60,000 cities across Europe and Asia.

Compare real-time prices from top railway companies and book the best-value tickets quickly, all while enjoying a smarter travel experience.

Why Choose Xmove?

  • Simple Interface: Secure tickets with a few taps
  • 28 Languages: Catered to users worldwide
  • Real-Time Updates: Check schedules and compare prices instantly
  • E-Ticket Service: Download tickets and manage orders effortlessly
  • Group Bookings: Enjoy customized discounts for families or groups
  • API Integration: Businesses can access railway data via our API
  • 24/7 Support: Expert assistance for all your booking needs
